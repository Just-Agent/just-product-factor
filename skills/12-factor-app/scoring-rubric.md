# 12-factor-app Scoring Rubric

Score each dimension from 0 to 10.

| Score | Meaning |
|---:|---|
| 0-2 | Missing or unusable |
| 3-4 | Present but fragile or unclear |
| 5-6 | Usable but incomplete |
| 7-8 | Strong and mostly production-ready |
| 9-10 | Excellent, polished, validated, and easy to adopt |
